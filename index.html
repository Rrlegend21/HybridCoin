What is HybridCoin?

HybridCoin (HYC) is a revolutionary cryptocurrency designed to combine the power of **AI computation** and **decentralized storage mining**. Built on the **Polygon blockchain**, HYC enables users to participate in energy-efficient mining that provides rewards for both **AI-based tasks** and **storage proof** contributions.

What Makes HYC Unique?

HybridCoin offers a unique mining experience by integrating **AI-driven mining** with **proof-of-storage**, reducing energy consumption while maximizing efficiency. Unlike traditional mining, HYC focuses on real-world utility, providing tangible rewards for computational and storage tasks. It's ideal for both miners and anyone interested in participating in the **decentralized computing economy**.

How to Mine HybridCoin (HYC)

Mining HybridCoin involves contributing **AI processing power** or **storage space** to the network. In exchange for your contribution, you will receive HYC tokens based on the amount of computational power and storage you provide. This is made possible through our secure and decentralized **Polygon-based network**.
